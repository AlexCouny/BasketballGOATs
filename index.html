Basketball history G.O.A.Ts

G.O.A.T

Michael Jordan

American basketball player and former NBA player. Played as an attacking defender. Jordan was instrumental in popularizing basketball and the NBA around the world in the 1980s and 1990s.

G.O.A.T

Kobe Bryant

American basketball player who played in the National Basketball Association for twenty seasons for one team, the Los Angeles Lakers. Played as an attacking defender. Drafted 13th overall.

G.O.A.T

Stephen "Chef" Curry

American professional basketball player who has played for the NBA's Golden State Warriors since 2009. Plays as point guard. Selected 7th overall in the 2009 NBA Draft. 4-time NBA champion, two-time NBA MVP.

G.O.A.T

Lebron "King" James

American basketball player who plays as a light and heavy forward. He plays for the Los Angeles Lakers NBA team. Recognized by experts as one of the best basketball players in history.
